An ancient entity granted form by the Archives of Prometheus. Unlike the Keepers, who guard knowledge, or the Scribes, who record it, the Wanderer exists for a singular purpose: exploration.

Bound neither to a single world nor a single story, the Archive Wanderer traverses countless realities preserved within the Prometheus Archives. It walks through empires long reduced to dust, witnesses romances that altered the fate of kingdoms, studies anomalies that defy reason, and listens to the final words of civilizations swallowed by time.
